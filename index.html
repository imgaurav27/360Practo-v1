Practo is a digital healthcare platform that connects patients with doctors, clinics, and pharmacies, offering services like online consultations, appointment booking, diagnostics, and medicine delivery. Practo is a healthcare technology company founded in 2008 with the mission to make quality healthcare accessible and affordable for over a billion people, primarily in India, and now operating in more than 22 countries. It provides a comprehensive medical directory of verified doctors, enabling users to search for healthcare professionals by specialty, experience, and location.


Key Services :
Online Consultations: Patients can consult doctors remotely across 20+ specialties, either through free or paid sessions.


Appointment Booking: Users can schedule appointments at over 9,000 hospitals and clinics, streamlining access to healthcare.


Medicine Delivery: Practo partners with verified pharmacies to deliver medicines directly to patients’ homes.


Diagnostic Services: Home sample collection for lab tests is available through Practo Associate Labs.

Practice Management Software: Tools like Ray and Insta help clinics and hospitals manage appointments, electronic medical records, and operational workflows.

Health Plans: Practo Plus offers subscription-based access to unlimited online consultations, free in-clinic visits, and health check-ups.

Technology and Security Practo leverages computer science, data science, and AI to improve healthcare outcomes, including AI-powered clinical decision support and telemedicine solutions. The platform ensures data security with 256-bit encryption, HIPAA-compliant data centers, and ISO 27001 certification.


Business Model : Practo operates primarily on a B2C model, connecting patients directly with healthcare providers. Revenue is generated through subscription fees, service charges, advertising, and performance-based booking fees. Healthcare providers also pay for better visibility on the platform, similar to search engine advertising. BIBS

Impact : With over 50 crore patients served and more than 5 lakh doctors and healthcare providers on its platform, Practo has become a major player in digital healthcare, improving accessibility, transparency, and continuity of care. Its services aim to reduce information asymmetry, improve affordability, and bring healthcare to the last mile through technology.
